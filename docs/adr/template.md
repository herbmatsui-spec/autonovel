# Architecture Decision Record (ADR) Template

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** [Proposed | Accepted | Superseded | Deprecated]
**Decider(s):** [Name/Role]

### 1. Context and Problem Statement
Describe the context and the problem being solved. What is the current situation? Why is a change or a decision necessary? Include any constraints or requirements.

### 2. Decision to be Made
Clearly state the decision that needs to be made. What are the primary goals?

### 3. Considered Options
List the alternatives that were evaluated.
- **Option 1: [Name]**
  - Pros: ...
  - Cons: ...
- **Option 2: [Name]**
  - Pros: ...
  - Cons: ...

### 4. Decision Outcome
**Chosen Option: [Option Name]**

**Justification:**
Explain why this option was chosen over the alternatives. How does it satisfy the goals and constraints mentioned in the Context section?

### 5. Consequences
Describe the impact of this decision.
- **Positive:** What improves?
- **Negative/Trade-offs:** What are the drawbacks? What new complexities are introduced?
- **Risks:** What could go wrong?

### 6. Alternatives’ Trade-offs (Optional)
Briefly explain why the other options were rejected.

### 7. Notes/References
Links to related ADRs, documentation, or external resources.
